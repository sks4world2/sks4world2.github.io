Small businesses in your interest


For your economic security and that of your next generation, you should encourage small businesses and preserve them. Big businesses and small businesses are like two sides of a coin. If one of them go away, it causes economic damage to you.

Imagine all the food items are sold only by a few big businesses. God forbid, if they turn against people, root for bigger profits and pollute the food market, compromise on quality and involve in price fixing - what would you do? Small vendors preserve the supply chain and they bail you out. If small businesses are erased, you and your next generation become powerless against corporates.
